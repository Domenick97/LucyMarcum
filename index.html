Lucy Marcum

Editor and Writer

Readily prepared to work in the writing and publishing industry, both of which will allow me to utilize my strong editorial, writing, and interpersonal skills. My previous experiences include literature editing, copyediting, article writing, freelance writing, and fact-checking. Graduated summa cum laude from NC State University with a BA in English/Creative Writing, minors in French and International Studies, and English Honors. Willing to relocate nationally and internationally.
